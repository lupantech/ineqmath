## Problem

Let $a, b, c$ be positive real numbers such that $abc = 1$. Find the largest constant $C$ such that the following inequality holds for all $a, b, c$:
$$
\frac{1}{a^5(b+2c)^2} + \frac{1}{b^5(c+2a)^2} + \frac{1}{c^5(a+2b)^2} \geq C.
$$
