## Problem

Let $a, b, c$ be positive real numbers. Find the largest positive constant $C$ such that the inequality below holds for all $a, b, c \in \mathbb{R}^{+}$:
$$
\frac{a}{(b+c)^2}+\frac{b}{(c+a)^2}+\frac{c}{(a+b)^2} \geq \frac{C}{a+b+c}
$$
