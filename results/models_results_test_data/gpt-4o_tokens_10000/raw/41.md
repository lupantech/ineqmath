## Problem

Let $\left(a_n\right)_{n \geq 0}$ be a sequence of real numbers such that for all $n \geq 0$,

$$
a_{n+1} \geq a_n^2 + \frac{1}{5}
$$

$$
\sqrt{a_{n+5}} \geq a_{n-5}^2 + C.
$$
